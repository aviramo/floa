Our Expertise

Seven domains, one focus

To enforce a comprehensive identity and access policy, we harness software from multiple domains into one complete solution.

IAM

Identity & Access Management

Automating the granting and removal of user permissions across all organizational systems, and managing user identity and authentication methods.

IGA

Identity Governance & Administration

Monitoring user activity against organizational data, controlling risk at access time, and detecting and handling permission anomalies.

PAM

Privileged Access Management

Managing and controlling privileged permissions and users with sensitive access, the most critical junction for securing the organization.

CIAM

Customer Identity Management

Identification and permissions for the organization's end customers, in a secure, convenient and seamless way that builds trust and loyalty.

DAM

Data Access Management

Governing access permissions to unstructured data and files, finding excessive access and reducing the attack surface.

IoT

Internet of Things

Managing the identities of things, identifying, authenticating and controlling access of devices and sensors connected to the organizational network.

AM

Access Management

Enforcing a unified access policy across systems and applications, single sign on (SSO) and multi factor authentication.

NHI

Non Human Identity Management

Controlling the identities of services, bots and interfaces, machine identities whose scope is growing and pose a hidden risk in every organization.
